.. _margarita:

Margarita
=========

Margarita is our library of
`Salt Stack <https://docs.saltstack.com/en/latest/>`_
states.  It lives at https://github.com/caktus/margarita.

.. warning::
    New projects should *not* use Margarita. We are using Tequila
    for new projects, or sometimes Fabulaws or other things. You
    can read more about picking deploy frameworks for projects at
    :ref:`deploy-strategies`.
